Big Idea 1 — Creative Development

In this unit, I practiced turning an idea into a working program by planning, testing, presenting, and explaining the design. I took notes and screenshots for documentation and made a short narration to summarize the purpose and how the program works.

  • 1.1 Collaboration: We divided tasks within the team; we used communication and simple version control to avoid blocking each other.
  • 1.2 Program Design & Development: I wrote simple algorithms and pseudocode; improved behavior with step-by-step testing and print checks.
  • 1.3 Program Function & Purpose: Defined the goal and audience; organized input-output flow; split logic into functions; documented everything for reuse.
  • 1.4 Identifying & Correcting Errors: Wrote small tests, tried edge cases, and added simple error handling to reduce crashes and invalid inputs.

2-minute narration: A short screen recording that summarizes the purpose, main features, and one debugging example. (See the video below.)
